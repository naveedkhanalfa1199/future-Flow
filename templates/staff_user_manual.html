1. System Overview

What is FuTuRe FLoW?

FuTuRe FLoW is a comprehensive university management system designed specifically for Study Advisers UK Islamabad. It helps staff members search universities, check student eligibility, generate professional advice reports, send advice on Email and copy for whatsapp to share with students.

Key Features:

  • Advanced University Database - Detailed requirements for each university
  • MOI Eligibility Checking - Automatic Medium of Instruction validation
  • Multiple Province Support - Universities with campuses in different provinces
  • Study Gap Filtering - Automatic filtering based on gap years
  • Bachelor Entry Requirements - FSc and BA marks validation
  • Professional PDF Reports - Ready-to-share advice documents
  • Multi-Criteria Search - Filter by country, province, level, and more

2. Login & Account Setup

For New Staff Members:

Step 1: Registration

  1. Visit the login page
  2. Click "Register as Staff"
  3. Fill in the registration form:
    • Userame - As per your official ID
    • Designation - As awarded by the company
    • Password - Minimum 8 characters recommended
    • Official Email - Your SAUK/Personal email address

Step 2: Verification

  1. After registration, complete the verification form
  2. Answer all 6 verification questions accurately
  3. Questions verify your employment with SAUK Islamabad
  4. Submit the form and wait for admin approval

Step 3: Approval

  1. Admin will review your verification answers
  2. Approval typically within 24-48 hours
  3. Once approved, login with your credentials
  4. Upon Approval you will get access to the user dashboard

4. Search Universities

This function can't download, Email or Copy options, it is only to check where the student is eligible:

  1. Click "Search Universities" from staff menu
  2. Apply filters as needed:
    • Country - Select from dropdown
    • Province - Appears after country selection
    • Subject - Field of study
    • Level - Bachelor/Master/PhD
    • Initial Deposit - Filter by deposit amount
  3. Click "Search" button
  4. View results in table format with complete details

Quick Tip:

Use Search Universities for quick lookups. For detailed student-specific advice with MOI checking and PDF generation, use Generate Advice feature instead.

5. Generate University Advice (MOST IMPORTANT)

This is your primary tool for advising students! Follow these steps carefully for best results.

Step-by-Step Process:

Step 1: Location Filters (Optional)

  • Country - Select if student has preference
  • Province - Automatically loads after country selection
  • Course Level - Bachelor/Master/PhD/etc

Step 2: Course Search (REQUIRED)

Enter the course name student is interested in:

  • Examples: "MBA", "Computer Science", "Nursing", "Civil Engineering"
  • System searches only in specific course names
  • Be specific for better results
  • Try variations if no results found

Step 3: Student Profile (HIGHLY RECOMMENDED)

Why Provide Student Profile?
  • Automatically checks MOI eligibility
  • Filters universities with exceeded study gap
  • Validates CGPA/percentage requirements
  • Shows only suitable universities
  • Saves time by removing unsuitable options
  • Provides accurate categorization (MOI vs Regular)
Enter Student Information:
Field Purpose Example
FSc English Marks (%) For MOI eligibility checking 70
Student CGPA Check eligibility (0-4 scale) 3.0
Student Percentage If no CGPA available 75
Study Gap Critical filter - calculates years since last degree 3 Years
BA Marks (%) Required for some bachelor programs 65
Last Degree University For MOI university-specific requirements NUST, LUMS

Step 4: Generate & View Results

Click "Generate Advice" button. System will process and show two categories:

Category 1: MOI Eligible Universities (GREEN BOX)

  • Student fully qualifies for MOI (Medium of Instruction)
  • NO English test required (No IELTS, PTE, TOEFL needed)
  • Profile meets all MOI requirements
  • TOP PRIORITY - Recommend these first to students

Category 2: Regular Universities (WHITE BOX)

  • English language test required (IELTS/PTE/TOEFL/etc.)
  • Student meets general CGPA requirements
  • Within study gap limits
  • Valid admission options if student has/can take English test

Step 5: Select Universities

  • 0 University is pre-selected by default
  • Use "Select All" or "Deselect All" buttons
  • Manually check/uncheck specific universities
  • Consider student's preferences: location, fee, scholarship
  • Review initial deposit requirements
  • Check English test requirements for regular universities

Step 6: Download PDF and Show Preview

  1. In the Show Preview button you can direclty click the copy button to get the advice ready to send on Whatsapp
  2. Click "Download PDF" button
  3. PDF will download automatically to your device
  4. Professional format ready to share with student
  5. Save copy for your records
Email and PDF Report Contains:
  • SAUK Islamabad official branding
  • Student greeting message
  • Total number of universities
  • Each university with complete details
  • Generated by: Your name (staff member)
  • Date and time stamp
  • Professional closing message

6. Tips & Best Practices

Before Student Meeting:

  1. Gather complete student information beforehand
  2. Verify all documents (FSc marksheet, BA degree, last degree)
  3. Calculate exact study gap (years between degrees)
  4. Note student's preferences: preferred country, budget constraints
  5. Ask about English language test status (already taken/planning to take)

During Advice Generation:

  1. Always provide complete student profile - more accurate results
  2. Review both result categories carefully
  3. Explain the difference between MOI and Regular admission clearly
  4. Discuss English test requirements for regular universities
  5. Consider student's timeline and test preparation capacity
  6. Prioritize MOI eligible universities for faster admissions

After Generating Report:

  1. Review PDF before sharing with student
  2. Discuss all options with student thoroughly
  3. Explain fee structures and payment schedules clearly
  4. Clarify scholarship availability and eligibility
  5. Save PDF copy for your records and follow-up
  6. Check the options by yourself for Afghan Students (free version don't have this filter)
  7. Must review the MOI options because you are on free version which do not contain extra verification features

Important Don'ts:

  • Don't share your login credentials with anyone
  • Don't select CGPA and Percentage both on same time, it may effect your the response
  • Don't send unnecessary emails, domain email might be blocked
  • Don't skip student profile when generating advice
  • Don't forget to logout after completing work
  • Don't ignore MOI Filters while looking for moi advice

8. Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions:

Issue: "Sorry we are working on it"

Solution: If you see such message so please wait because admin might be working on this feature.

Issue: "Account is locked"

Reason: When System detects the wrong password attempts

Solution: Whatsapp admin 03016290781. Do not attempt more logins otherwise your profile will be locked permanantly.

Issue: "No universities found"

Confirm:

  • Use Less filters if need more number of universities
  • Try broader search terms (e.g., "Business" instead of "MBA International Business")
  • For MOI must use moi filters
  • Use maximum filters to find the exact option

Issue: "Email not sent"

Solutions:

  • Must select the universities from the checkbox
  • Confirm that the email address exsits
  • Check your internet connection
  • Clear browser cache and try again

Issue: "PDF not downloading"

Solutions:

  • Ensure at least one university is selected (checkbox checked)
  • Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
  • Check browser pop-up blocker settings
  • Ensure sufficient storage space on device
  • Wait a few seconds - large PDFs take time

Issue: "Internal Server Error"

Solutions:

  • Move back to any option, refresh the page and then try again
  • If error persists, Whatsapp admin 03016290781
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Try accessing from different browser/device
